Student Section

The Student Section is a group of HCHS students ranging from 15-20 people. Their main priority at the games is to hype up the football team and to make the game more enjoyable. They paint their bodies, play music, and yell to make the game a better experience for students and the people attending the games.

Senior Cody Wilson, the leader of the student section, says "If you are willing to participate and cheer, it's one of the most fun, but if you sit on your phone it won't be fun."
