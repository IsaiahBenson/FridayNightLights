Tiger Motion

The award winning Tiger Motion Dance Team is a group composed of 10 members. They dance on the sidelines at football games to hype up both the crowd and football team, as well as other school events.

Tiger Motion captain, Tatum Hogan, "Being on the dance team is hard work, but it is my favorite part of high school."
