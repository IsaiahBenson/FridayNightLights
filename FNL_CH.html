Cheer

The Cheer Team does an outstanding job of cheering on our football, and our basketball teams. They dance on the sidelines and even perform at Homecoming. Captain is Gracie Young, and Co-Captains are Alyssa Austin, and Megan Screws. They will be attending the Citrus Bowl later this year, and perfoming in the pre-game.

Senior. Co-Captain Megan Screws says, "Being on the cheer team is a ton of hard work. We have such a strong bond and we are like a family."
