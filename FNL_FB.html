The Tigers

The Hardin County Football team, "The Tigers", is known for beating almost every opposing school. The Tigers are a talented and hardworking team, and the best in southwest Tennessee.

Rivers Hunt has this to say about the football team, "Hard work beats talent, when talent doesn't work hard."
