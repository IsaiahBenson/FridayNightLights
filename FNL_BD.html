The Pride

'The Pride' is one of the largest school and community organizations. They are strong supporters of football, basketball, and other school organizations. Band teaches students the importance of the arts as well as teamwork and personal responsibility.

Senior Ben Brown has this to say about 'The Pride', "With all the hard work, time, and perseverance that goes into this organization, the band has rightfully earned the title of 'The Pride of Hardin County'."
